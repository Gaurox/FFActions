# Code of Conduct

This project aims to stay practical, respectful, and welcoming.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that help keep the project healthy:

- being respectful in discussion and review
- giving clear technical feedback without hostility
- assuming good intent while staying honest about problems
- focusing on the work rather than on the person

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- deliberate intimidation
- disruptive or bad-faith behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

## Scope

This applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, and other public project spaces.
